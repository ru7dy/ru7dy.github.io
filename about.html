Hey there, I'm Rodrigo; I am an University of Valladolid student of Telecommunications engineering degree, specialized in Telematics field.

I started blogging using Wordpress in 2007, and I closed my first blog in 2011. After that, I've been changing my blog site very often, mostly using Blogger and its easy integration on custom domains. However, I'm tired of WYSIWYG blogs, so I've decided to change and use Jekyll.

Translations

I started practising ROM hacking when I was really young, changing texts and tiles of GBC Pok√©mon games. I used to use tools to edit their memory and change its values. Also I did some changes just editing the game with a simple hexadecimal editor.

After that, my first big project was the spanish fan-made localization of The World Ends With You, a role-playing NDS game. Our team analyzed the game, reversed the text, offset, sounds, images... And we created tools to edit it. It had a really big impact on the scene these days.

IlDucci continued this localization and he recently released a new re-localization, re-dubbing every character more professionaly and finishing the translation of half of the graphics we couldn't edit back in the day. For further information, have a look here.

Also, I've contributed to the spanish fan-made localization of Chrono Trigger, a remake of a Square Enix classic role-playing game for NDS. For further information, have a look here.

Reversing and scripting

I love reversing stuff and exploring how is it working. I started to get serious reversing stuff a couple of years ago, when I used to play League of Legends. I got into some reversing engineering forums looking for bots and I started learning how to develop my own one.

Meanwhile, I developed plenty of open source scripts for the game for Bot Of Legends, a well-known tool to use scripts in the game, and LeagueSharp, a relatively new tool which supports C# instead of LUA.

Notwithstanding, none of them are still on my GitHub: Some of them were outdated, some got lost when I changed my GitHub user half a year ago.
